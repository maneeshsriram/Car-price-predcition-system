Project Overview

Buying a new car is a still a dream for a large section of the society. With the increasing price and taxes, buying a new vehicle becomes even more difficult. With no option left, people have to switch to buy a second hand car.
But the customers can be exploited by fixing an unrealistic price. ​Therefore, rises an absolute necessity of a used car price prediction system to effectively determine the worthiness of the car using a variety of features.​

This project mainly focuses on predicting the worthiness of the used car. The users of the website can be customers, who would want to know the price of the car they are about to buy or the sellers, who would want to know at what price their car can be sold.
